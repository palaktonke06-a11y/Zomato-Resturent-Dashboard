📊 Project Name: Zomato Restaurant Strategy & Consumer Analytics Dashboard

📝 Project Overview
This project addresses the operational and market positioning challenges faced in the highly competitive food aggregator and restaurant space. The objective was to consolidate extensive restaurant listings, user engagement metrics, and regional dining behaviors into a centralized intelligence hub. By designing this interactive dashboard, I provided a data-driven solution that allows business stakeholders to analyze cuisine trends, evaluate online ordering vs. table booking demographics, understand geographic distribution, and cross-examine dining costs against consumer satisfaction ratings.

🚀 Key Features & Insights
Market Share & Culinary Mapping: Identifies the volume footprint across cities and highlights the top-performing cuisines capturing the market.
Operational Readiness Auditing: Tracks consumer adoption of key service offerings like table bookings and online deliveries.
Cost-Value Matrix: Cross-analyzes average meal costs for two people against restaurant aggregate ratings to uncover optimal price segments.

1. Restaurant Distribution & Feature Overview

Visuals Breakdown:
KPI Summary Cards: Tracks high-level aggregate targets including Total Restaurants (9.536K), Average Rating metrics (25.42K), and Total Votes compiled (1M).
Horizontal Bar Chart (City Wise Restaurant Count): Ranks regional presence, clearly isolating New Delhi as the largest market hub, followed by Gurgaon and Noida.
Pie Chart (Online Delivery Analysis): Breaks down the share of listings that offer delivery features, revealing that 74.31% do not offer online delivery compared to 25.69% that do.
Donut Chart (Table Booking Analysis): Highlights feature availability, showing that an overwhelming 87.86% of restaurants do not support table bookings while 12.14% do.
Vertical Column Chart (Top 10 Cuisines): Identifies consumer options by tracking restaurant counts per cuisine, with North Indian scaling as the absolute market leader.
Interactive Slicer List (City): A vertical checkbox selector enabling immediate workspace filtering across international and local markets like Abu Dhabi, Agra, Ahmedabad, and Albany.

2. Cost Dynamics & Performance Metrics

Visuals Breakdown:
Scatter Plot (Average Cost for Two vs Rating): Maps individual restaurant positions by plotting aggregate ratings against the average cost for two to spotlight cost-efficiency clusters.
Funnel Chart (City by Price Range): Displays a tiered volume distribution model charting down restaurant listings across predefined cost-tier classifications.
Clustered Column Chart (Price Range vs Average Rating): Directly evaluates whether higher price bands match higher consumer ratings across performance thresholds.
Horizontal Bar Chart (Top 10 Restaurants by Votes): Ranks prominent restaurants by total customer engagement metrics in key metropolitan hubs.
Area Chart (City Restaurants Counts): Smoothly charts density slopes of listings across localized markets to reveal supply volumes.
Detailed Operations Table: A granular matrix grid providing precise row-level auditing of specific venues (e.g., Denny's, Famous Dave's Barbecue, Pizza Di Rocco) across their exact Localities, Vote volumes, Price Ranges, and Average Cost for Two.

🛠️ Tech Stack Used
Analytics Tool: Power BI Desktop
Data Source: Zomato Restaurant Global Listings Dataset (Excel / CSV)
DAX Functions & Metrics: Count of Restaurant ID, Sum of Aggregate Rating, Total Votes Distribution, Feature Delivery Percentages, and Price Tier Segmentation.
